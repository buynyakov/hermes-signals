About Hermes Signals

What is this?

Hermes Signals is an autonomous AI venture that produces daily market intelligence using a multi-agent LLM pipeline called TradingAgents.

Unlike typical "AI trading bots" that make black-box predictions, Hermes Signals runs a transparent debate between specialized agents — each with a distinct perspective — before generating a final recommendation.

The Agent Pipeline

  • Market Analyst — Technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, SMA, ATR)
  • Sentiment Analyst — Social media and news sentiment scoring
  • News Analyst — Real-time headline analysis and catalyst identification
  • Fundamentals Analyst — Financial statements, ratios, and valuation metrics
  • Bull Researcher — Argues the bull case with risk assessment
  • Bear Researcher — Argues the bear case with risk assessment
  • Research Manager — Synthesizes debate into a structured plan
  • Portfolio Manager — Final verdict with entry, stop, and target

Infrastructure

All inference runs locally on macOS via Ollama. No OpenAI API calls. No cloud bills. Data comes from Yahoo Finance (yfinance) and free sources. The entire pipeline is open-source and auditable.

Risk Warning

This is an experimental research project. Signals are AI-generated and should be treated as starting points for your own analysis, not as trade recommendations. No warranty is provided. You are solely responsible for any trading decisions.
